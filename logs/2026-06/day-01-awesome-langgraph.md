## Q&A — Why This Contribution

**Q: Why target this repo for a first contribution?**
A: Curated "awesome-*" lists are designed to grow by community additions. The CONTRIBUTING.md explicitly invites project entries. The bar is: project must use LangChain/LangGraph, description under 200 chars, correct format. All three met. Merge rate is near 100% for genuine, on-topic entries.

**Q: Why these two projects specifically?**
A: They cover the two hottest topics in the repo — RAG pipelines and agent systems. They have concrete, differentiating features (HyDE, CRAG, semantic cache, YAML agent config) that make the descriptions stand out rather than being generic.

**Q: What skills does this demonstrate to a recruiter who sees it?**
A: (1) Chandan contributes to open source. (2) His repos are good enough to be listed in a curated index alongside 80+ other community projects. (3) He knows the LangChain/LangGraph ecosystem well enough to place his work in the right section.

**Q: What if the maintainer asks for changes?**
A: Common asks: alphabetical order within section, reformat description. Both are quick fixes. Respond within 24h and the PR gets merged.
